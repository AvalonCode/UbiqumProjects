The Store

Our story began in 1999, when founder Alana Lyndis wandered the streets of San Francisco looking for a place to buy some candy for her little brother Roy, only to find that there wasn't a single one! After giving it some thought she decided to open her own shop, where children could find any kind of sweet they craved, and we have been spreading happiness ever since.

The business saw massive success, and Alana was able to open more stores around the country. After 20 wonderful years, you can now find SweetShop stores in San Francisco, New York and Washington DC.

Products

Candy

You can't call yourself a candy shop without candy! We have all kinds, including gummy bears, candy canes, lollipops, bubblegum, cotton candy, jelly beans, toppings. You name it, we got it!

And only the best quality too, of course: we hand-pick the best products from the best manufacturers to bring them directly to you at an affordable price. If you want to see for yourself come to one of our stores and grab some!

Chocolate

Chocolate. Do I need to say more? Regular, dark, white, with nuts, the choice is yours to make.

Not only do we have a variety in types, we have variety in shapes too: bars, chips, flakes, bombons, and even frogs! (You know, like the ones from Harry Potter. They don't move though).

Cakes & Pies

Have a slice of one our delicious cakes. See that amazing raspberry cheesecake in the pic to the left? That one's our specialty.

By the way, from time to time we have events where we teach attendees how to bake our cakes, check the Events section down below if you're interested!

Cupcakes

If our cakes are too big for you worry not, because we also have cupcakes. These little guys taste just like they look, and they look gorgeous if I do say so myself.

Honestly, this stuff sells itself so I'll only add that we also have events where you can learn some cool cupcake recipes. Again, check the details below in the Events section.
