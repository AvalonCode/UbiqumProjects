August 4th: NYSL Fundraiser

On August the 4th we will be hosting our yearly Fundraiser! Just like previous years, we need to collect funds so that we can make the Northside Youth Soccer League the BEST Junior League in Chicago!

All parents whose children play in our League's teams will be invited (along with the kids, of course!) to the event, as well as parents who want to enroll their children in the League and anyone who wants to volunteer or help with the many tasks the League requires. See you all there!

August 16th: Season Kick-Off

It's party time! On August 16th the new season of the Northside Youth Soccer League officially begins!

Everyone is invited to our party: we will have lots of food and drinks to feed our young athletes (and their parents, don't worry ;D). Come to have fun and meet all the teams of the season!

September 1st: First Match

Summer is over, now it's time to get serious! Remember this date, because on September the 1st takes place the first match of the Season!

I could give you more details right now, but I won't. Why, you ask? Because of our new Schedule page! Head over there to find a table with any and all the info you need for all the matches of the season. Make sure to check it out so that you don't miss a single one!
